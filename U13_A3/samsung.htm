About Samsung:

Samsung is a global leader in technology and innovation, headquartered in South Korea. The company is renowned for its wide range of high-quality products, including smartphones (Galaxy series), smart TVs, home appliances, wearable devices, and cutting-edge semiconductors. Samsung is also at the forefront of developing advanced technologies like foldable displays, 5G connectivity, and AI-powered solutions.

In addition to consumer electronics, Samsung offers memory chips, OLED displays, and components that power many other brands' devices. Its diverse portfolio extends to smart home ecosystems, digital health, and enterprise solutions. Samsung’s commitment to innovation, sustainability, and user-friendly design ensures it remains a top choice for consumers and businesses worldwide.
