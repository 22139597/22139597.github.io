About Apple:

Apple Inc. is a world-leading technology company based in Cupertino, California, celebrated for its innovation, design excellence, and seamless integration of hardware, software, and services. Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple has transformed industries with groundbreaking products like the iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and AirPods.

Beyond hardware, Apple offers powerful software platforms including iOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS, enabling a cohesive and intuitive user experience. The company also provides a suite of services such as the App Store, Apple Music, Apple TV+, iCloud, Apple Arcade, and Apple Pay, delivering entertainment, productivity, and convenience. Known for its commitment to quality, privacy, and sustainability, Apple continues to redefine how technology enhances everyday life
