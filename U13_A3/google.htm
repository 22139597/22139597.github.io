About Google:

Google LLC is a global technology leader, best known for its internet services, software, and innovative hardware products. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Google revolutionized how people access and interact with information through its search engine, Google Search. The company has since expanded its offerings to include popular services such as Gmail, Google Maps, YouTube, Google Drive, and Google Cloud, alongside a range of consumer devices.

In addition to software, Google has made significant strides in the hardware market with its Pixel smartphones. Known for their advanced AI features, cutting-edge cameras, and seamless integration with Google’s ecosystem, Pixel phones deliver a premium user experience. Each new Pixel model showcases the latest Android updates, powerful processing capabilities, and smart features, such as Google Assistant and real-time translation. With a focus on innovation and ease of use, Google continues to shape the future of both mobile technology and digital services.
