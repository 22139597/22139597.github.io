UNIT 1-2

In these units, I learned how websites and emails work, how devices share information, and how online services like cloud storage, blogs, and search engines are used in everyday life. I also found out how to stay safe and behave responsibly online. I explored how different digital devices connect through the internet and how technology is becoming more connected with smart systems. I also looked at the risks of using the internet and how to protect personal information. In Unit 2, I learned how technology systems work. I studied hardware parts like screens, printers, processors, memory, and buses. I also learned how software like operating systems, utility programs, and apps help run computers and support everyday tasks. I understood how networks allow devices to communicate and how data moves inside a computer. These units gave me a strong understanding of how technology systems and the online world work, which is useful for many IT careers and future learning in other IT topics.

About Unit 1-2

"The Online World" Exploring the Digital Landscape Content: This unit covers things like Online Services and communication, How documents can be used online, keeping documents secure from hackers and using Social Media.

"Computing in the Workplace" Preparing for IT Job Roles Content: This unit covers thingss like different job roles in ICT workplaces, what a realistic workplace environment is like, Legislation and Policies and how to use Social Media in the Workplace.

“Technology Systems” Understanding Core IT Infrastructure Content: This unit covers fundamental components of IT systems, such as computer hardware, software, and networks. It explores how different technologies work together to create functional systems, how data is processed, and how users interact with these systems. Additionally, I also learned about maintaining and troubleshooting technology systems to ensure smooth operation

“Hardware” Understanding and Managing Physical Components of IT Systems Content: This unit dives into the world of computer hardware, including the types and functions of various components like processors, memory, storage devices, and peripherals. It also covers how to assemble, configure, and maintain hardware. I also explored the best practices in hardware safety, upgrading systems, and the impact of hardware on overall system performance.
