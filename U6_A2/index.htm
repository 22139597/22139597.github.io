Welcome to Celtec Games

Celtec Games is an indie game studio based in Wrexham, North East Wales. We create fun and original games for desktop, mobile, and all major consoles.

What We Do

Our team is made up of creative people who love making games. We work together to build exciting worlds and experiences for players of all ages. Whether you play on your phone, computer, or console, we have something for you.

Our Goal

We want to make games that are not just entertaining, but also spark your imagination. By using new technology and fresh ideas, we aim to deliver games that everyone can enjoy, no matter where they play.
