About Us

Celtec Games is an indie game studio based in Wrexham, North East Wales. We create fun and original games for desktop, mobile, and all major consoles.

Our Story

We started in 2020. A small team of gamers and makers built us. We wanted to create cool games for all players. Now, we are a studio in Wales. We love games and try new ideas.

Our Team

Our team likes making games. We have artists, coders, writers, and designers. We work as a group to make fun worlds for kids and adults. Play on any device, we have games for you.

Our Goal

We make games that are fun and help you dream. We use new tech and ideas. Our games work for everyone, no matter how you play.
